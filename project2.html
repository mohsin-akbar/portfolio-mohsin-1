Features Of MyBlog App

User Authentication and Profiles

User registration and login & Profiles

Sign-up and login functionality. Password recovery and reset. Role-based access control (e.g., admin, editor, reader). User Profiles Profile picture, bio, and social media links. Option for users to follow/unfollow each other.

Blog Post Management

Create, edit, delete, and view blog posts. Rich text editor for formatting posts (bold, italics, lists, links, etc.). Categories and tags for organizing posts.

Comments and Interactions

Allow readers to comment on posts. Reply to comments and threaded discussions. Like or dislike posts and comments.

Media Management

Upload and manage images, videos, and other media files. Embed media within blog posts.

Search and Filtering

Full-text search for posts and comments. Filter posts by category, tag, author, and date.

Notifications

Email or in-app notifications for new comments, likes, and follows. Push notifications for mobile users.

Social Sharing

Share posts on social media platforms (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.). Social login options.

Collaborative Blogging

Allow multiple authors to collaborate on a single post. Author credits and bylines

Performance Optimization

Caching strategies for faster page loads. Image optimization (e.g., lazy loading, compression).
